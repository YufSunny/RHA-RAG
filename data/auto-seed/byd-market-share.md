# BYD — Market Share & Global Position

**Source:** Public press releases (BYD Global), CPCA / CAAM monthly
reports, Statista compilations, CnEVPost reporting. All figures
are publicly disclosed; none are proprietary or restricted.

## 1. Global plug-in EV market share, 2025

| Manufacturer | Market share |
|---|---|
| BYD | 19% |
| Tesla | 9% |
| VW Group | 5% |
| Geely–Volvo Car Group | 5% |
| SAIC | 4% |
| Others | 58% |

BYD became the world's #1 electric vehicle maker in 2025 with
2.26 million BEVs sold, overtaking Tesla (1.64 million BEVs).

## 3. China vs overseas split (BYD own sales)

| Year | China sales | Overseas sales | Overseas % |
|---|---|---|---|
| 2022 | 1,725,108 | 77,356 | 4.3% |
| 2023 | 2,850,000 | 174,417 | 5.8% |
| 2024 | 4,050,000 | 417,000 | 9.3% |
| 2025 | 3,500,000 | 1,040,000 | 22.9% |

Overseas sales grew 200%+ YoY in 2025 driven by Europe and Latin
America. Key overseas markets in 2025: UK 51,422 units, Spain 15,857,
Germany 23,306, Italy ~3,000–4,000/month, Norway 5,927.

## 4. Why BYD overtook Tesla on BEVs in 2024

- Vertical integration: in-house blade battery, e-Platform 3.0.
- Broad model lineup (A00 Seagull → U9 supercar) vs Tesla's narrow
  Model 3 / Y / S / X / Cybertruck range.
- Strong PHEV / DM-i portfolio (2.49M in 2024) that Tesla does not
  compete in.
- Aggressive pricing after the Feb 2024 price war.

## 5. Caveats

Quarterly overseas split for 2024 is BYD's own disclosure; CPCA
retail share is passenger-only and excludes commercial vehicles.
