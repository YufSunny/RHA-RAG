# Ford Scheduled Maintenance — Light-Duty (excerpt)

**Source:** Ford Motor Company owner's manual and service-maintenance
schedule, public excerpt. Applies to North American models 2018+.

## Table 1 — Service intervals (miles)

| Service item | Normal use | Severe use |
|---|---|---|
| Engine oil + filter | 10,000 mi | 5,000 mi |
| Cabin air filter | 20,000 mi | 15,000 mi |
| Engine air filter | 30,000 mi | 20,000 mi |
| Spark plugs (2.0L EcoBoost) | 100,000 mi | 60,000 mi |
| Spark plugs (5.0L V8) | 100,000 mi | 60,000 mi |
| Coolant (orange OAT) | 150,000 mi | 100,000 mi |
| Transmission fluid (10R80) | 150,000 mi | 100,000 mi |
| Brake fluid | 3 years | 3 years |

## Section 2 — What counts as "severe use"

Per Ford, severe use includes any of: sustained towing above 50%
GVWR, repeated short trips under 5 miles in temperatures below 0°F,
extensive idling (taxi / delivery), or frequent dusty / off-road
conditions.

## Section 3 — Key indicator (KAM) reset

After any service that resets the oil-life monitor (oil change,
filter, etc.), perform the KAM reset via the dash menu to clear the
service reminder. The procedure is vehicle-specific; consult the
owner's manual for the exact sequence.
