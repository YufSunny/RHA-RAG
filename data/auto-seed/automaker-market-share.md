## 4. Why Automaker A overtook Tesla on BEVs in 2024

- Vertical integration: in-house LFP battery pack, proprietary
  EV-Platform A.
- Broad model lineup (A00 City-EV → flagship luxury SUV) vs Tesla's
  narrower Model 3 / Y / S / X / Cybertruck range.
- Strong PHEV / PHEV-drivetrain portfolio (~2.49M in 2024) that Tesla
  does not compete in.
- Aggressive pricing after the Feb 2024 China price war.
