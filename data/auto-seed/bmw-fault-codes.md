# BMW Fault-Code Glossary (excerpt)

**Source:** BMW Group service-information public reference. Manufacturer
codes appear alongside the generic OBD-II P-code where applicable.

## 2A78 — VANOS intake, mechanical fault

BMW-specific. Indicates the variable valve-timing solenoid on the
intake camshaft is not reaching commanded position. Generic equivalent
on some models is **P0011**.

## 2A82 — DME internal error, EEPROM checksum

BMW-specific. The Digital Motor Electronics (DME) module failed its
self-test. Often resolved by reprogramming; persistent cases require
module replacement.

## 30FF — Hybrid system: high-voltage interlock open

BMW i-series and ActiveHybrid models. The high-voltage service
interlock loop is open — DO NOT drive. Inspect the orange interlock
connectors before reconnecting 12V.

## P0301 — Cylinder 1 Misfire Detected (generic)

Same as the OBD-II generic code. On BMW N20 / B48 engines, frequently
traced to failed ignition coil (single coil replacement, not the full
set) or carbon-fouled injector.

## P0420 — Catalyst Efficiency Below Threshold (Bank 1)

Same as the generic OBD-II code. BMW N20 and B48 catalysts are known
to degrade after ~100k miles, particularly in markets with leaded
fuel contamination history.
